About Me

What can I tell you about myself...

Hi! My name is Rashid. I'm a bay area born and raised individual. I grew up in a little suburn town in the east bay area. All my life I thought I'd get into the medical field. I attended a medical high school to further pursue those dreams. But once I attended college, I realized that technology is where my heart desired to be. The earlist memory I can recall of using a computer is when I was around 8 years old. My cousin was very into coding and I couldnt resist watching over him. I always thoguht it was an interesting thing to watch.
During high school I was a hard learner and my grades and GPA reflected that. Fast forward to college, I had a rough first year. Not only did I fail my first computer science class requirement, it was an eye opener to myself. I realized computer science wasn't only about memorization, it was about real life learning experiences. Since then, I devoted more of my time into learning the methods of computer science and the underlying meaning of coding; Optimization, cleanliness/organization, and much more. With the conclusion of my senior year, I've learned a lot. Made a lot of project, learned the use of different methods, API's, systems, and found my strengths and weaknesses.
Continuing my career after college is going to be an interesting change of pace. From coding for classes and projects to coding for companies, apps, bigger projects, I can personally say I can improve and learn more from any career path I find myself in. I hope one day to find a career where I can use my strengths of C++/C, Java, Shell Scripting or Mobile App Development.

My name is Rashid Goshtasbi.
I hope one day you find what you love to do and never live a day regretting it.

-Rashid Shahmardan Goshtasbi
